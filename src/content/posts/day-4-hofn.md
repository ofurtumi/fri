Jökulsárlón in the morning light was the first moment of the trip where we both just went quiet. Icebergs the size of houses drifting out to sea, seals weaving between them like it's nothing.

Höfn for dinner. The langoustine situation got out of hand financially, and I regret nothing.

The rattle from day 2 turned out to be a loose heat shield. One hose clamp from the toolbox and the van purrs again. Breakdown counter stays at one, on a technicality.
